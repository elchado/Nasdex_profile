    • Company Description


      The blockchain-based crowdfunding mechanism popularly known as initial coin offering (ICO) is allowing small and medium enterprises get the funding they need to grow and expand from investors all over the world who are participating in exchange for digital tokens. As this blockchain-based solution begins to flourish and gain popularity, the lack of transparency and regulatory certainty is becoming an issue as it leaves participants uncomfortably vulnerable to fraudulent events. We propose a solution to the lack of formality by establishing a platform that vets each one of the participants involved and that adheres to federal regulations in order to set proper guidelines to ensure a transparent and safe investment ecosystem.
